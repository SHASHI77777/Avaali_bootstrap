About Us

Avaali is a prominent digital solutions provider, specializing in cost-effective solutions for upper mid to large enterprises. With over 160 successful engagements across Asia, MEA, and parts of Europe, Avaali has demonstrated expertise in shared service automation, delivering significant reductions in process cycle time (35-50%) and costs (10-15%). Operating under Velocious, Avaali offers both product lines and consulting services in collaboration with various technology vendors, focusing on improved visibility, governance, and transparency. Notably, Avaali is a leader in procure-to-pay automation, boasting over 100 successful implementations in this domain.
